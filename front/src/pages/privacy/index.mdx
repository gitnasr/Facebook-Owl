**Privacy Policy for Facebook Owl Chrome Extension**

**Last updated: March 2024**

Thank you for using Facebook Owl Chrome Extension! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension.

**1. Information We Collect**

- **Cookies:** Facebook Owl Chrome Extension may use cookies to enhance user experience. Users can choose to set their web browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If they do so, note that some parts of the extension may not function properly.
- **Friend List from Facebook:** Facebook Owl Chrome Extension may access your Facebook Friends List to provide functionalities such as tracking, syncing, and analyzing your friends. This information is collected with user consent and is used solely for the intended purposes of the extension.

**2. How We Use Your Information**

- **End-to-End Encryption:** Any data collected, including friend lists from Facebook, is protected by end-to-end encryption. This ensures that your information remains secure and inaccessible to unauthorized parties.
- **Comparison of Friend Lists:** The collected friend lists are used internally by our servers to perform comparisons and provide the desired functionalities of the Facebook Owl Chrome Extension.

**3. Data Sharing**

We do not share any collected data, including friend lists from Facebook, with third parties. Your privacy and security are our top priorities, and we are committed to keeping your information confidential.

**4. Data Protection**

Your information is protected by robust security measures, including end-to-end encryption, to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your personal information.

**5. Cookies and Tracking**

Facebook Owl Chrome Extension does not use any cookies or tracking technologies.

**6. Opt-Out Options**

Currently, there are no options provided within the extension for users to opt-out of data collection or tracking.

**7. Contact Us**

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or the handling of your data, please contact us at gitnasr@proton.me.

**Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

By using Facebook Owl Chrome Extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
